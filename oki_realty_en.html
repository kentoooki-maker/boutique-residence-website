Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Boutique Residence & Co. Inc. (the "Company") recognises the protection of personal information as an important responsibility, and complies with the Act on the Protection of Personal Information and all other applicable laws and regulations.

01 — Collection of Personal Information

The Company collects personal information such as names, contact details, and property information only to the extent necessary for handling enquiries, property consultations, and brokerage transactions. Collection is carried out through lawful and fair means, with the purpose of use disclosed to the individual in advance.

02 — Purpose of Use

Personal information obtained will be used solely for the following purposes. It will not be used beyond the stated scope.

  • · Conducting real estate sales and brokerage services and related communications
  • · Providing property and market information (with consent)
  • · Responding to enquiries
  • · Fulfilling obligations required by law

03 — No Disclosure to Third Parties

The Company will not provide or disclose personal information to any third party, except in the following cases.

  • · With the consent of the individual
  • · When disclosure is required by law
  • · When necessary to protect life, body, or property and consent is difficult to obtain

04 — Security Management

The Company implements appropriate organisational and technical security measures to prevent leakage, loss, or damage of personal information, and ensures proper supervision of all personnel handling such information.

05 — Rights of Access, Correction & Deletion

Individuals have the right to request disclosure, correction, addition, deletion, or suspension of use of their personal information held by the Company. Please contact us using the details below. Requests will be handled within a reasonable period following identity verification.

06 — Compliance

The Company complies with the Act on the Protection of Personal Information, the Ministry of Land, Infrastructure, Transport and Tourism's guidelines on personal information protection in the real estate industry, and all other applicable laws and guidelines, and will continually improve its privacy practices.
